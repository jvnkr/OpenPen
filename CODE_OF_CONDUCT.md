# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want OpenPen to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level or background.

## Standards

Be respectful and constructive. Not tolerated: harassment, personal attacks,
demeaning or discriminatory comments, or sharing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainers via a
private channel (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the reporting flow).
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate
this code, and may ban repeat or serious offenders.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
